Membership Information

Thank you for your interest in joining the Lexington Community Band! We welcome musicians of all skill levels who share a passion for creating and performing music.

Our band is a supportive and friendly environment where you can grow as a musician and connect with fellow enthusiasts. We encourage anyone interested to attend a rehearsal to see if our band is a good fit for you.

For more information on membership requirements, dues, and how to join, please contact us. We look forward to making music with you!
