Rehearsal Schedule

The Lexington Community Band typically rehearses once a week to prepare for upcoming performances. Our rehearsals are a blend of focused work and enjoyable camaraderie.

We strive to create a productive environment where musicians can hone their skills and contribute to the collective sound of the band. Regular attendance is encouraged to ensure we are well-prepared for our concerts.

Please check this page regularly for updates to our rehearsal schedule, as times and locations may occasionally vary. We look forward to seeing you at our next rehearsal!

When

Rehearsals take place every Tuesday evening from 7:30-9:00 PM.

We begin rehearsing approximately 3 months prior to our fall and spring concerts. Please check the Calendar page for specific dates.

Where

The Lexington Community Band rehearses in the River Bluff High School band room:

320 Corley Mill Rd,
Lexington, SC 29072
